# Founder Studio Cover Design

## Approved Direction

Use a mobile-first "Visual Systems Odyssey" direction for the personal site.

The site should feel like a premium creative studio presence, not an old-fashioned portfolio or a traditional CV. The first screen should make Cristhian Guerrero Zaldivar and FlowMind Studio feel clear, current, professional, cinematic, and visually memorable.

## Hero

- Lead with `Cristhian Guerrero Zaldivar` as the main visual signal.
- Position him as `Founder and Technical Director at FlowMind Studio`.
- Use a strong editorial portrait composition inside a generated cinematic scene.
- Keep place/culture references visual and atmospheric, not literal in the copy.
- Show project previews as floating visual artifacts inside the hero scene.
- Use generated transparent layers for depth and subtle motion.
- Keep the mobile version as the primary design target, not a compressed desktop layout.
- Use short, confident copy:
  - `Founder and Technical Director building premium digital presence through FlowMind Studio.`
  - `I design and build polished websites, local SEO systems, and public-facing digital experiences for real businesses that need to look trustworthy from the first scroll.`

## Navigation

- Use concise navigation labels: `Studio`, `Work`, `Capabilities`, `Contact`.
- Remove resume-first framing from the primary navigation.

## Visual Direction

- Replace the generic light portfolio mood with a custom cinematic visual scene.
- Use generated coastal/light imagery as the hero stage.
- Use transparent generated overlays for glass, botanical forms, data trails, and depth.
- Use coral, mango, turquoise, deep ink, and warm cream.
- Avoid making the hero feel like a dashboard, stock template, or dark portfolio shell.

## Generated Assets

- `public/assets/hero/tropical-coast.png`: generated coastal hero backdrop.
- `public/assets/hero/glass-wave.png`: generated chroma-key glass wave converted to alpha PNG.
- `public/assets/hero/tropical-frame.png`: generated chroma-key botanical/data frame converted to alpha PNG.

## Content Cleanup

- Remove direct phone/contact-number links from public UI, generated resume, PDF, and structured data.
- Remove named non-informatics employers and any work history unrelated to informatics.
- Replace traditional `Experience` framing with studio/work/practice framing where appropriate.

## Calls To Action

- Primary: `Visit FlowMind Studio`
- Secondary: `See work`
- Contact: `Email me`

## Success Criteria

- The first viewport should feel premium and current on mobile.
- The site should feel more like a creative technical studio presence than a CV.
- No direct phone number, direct-message contact link, named non-informatics employer, or non-informatics work history should remain in public-facing content.
