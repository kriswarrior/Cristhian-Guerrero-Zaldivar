# Founder Studio Cover Design

## Approved Direction

Use a mobile-first "Founder Studio Cover" direction for the personal site.

The site should feel like a premium creative studio presence, not an old-fashioned portfolio or a traditional CV. The first screen should make Cristhian Guerrero Zaldivar and FlowMind Studio feel clear, current, professional, and visually memorable.

## Hero

- Lead with `Cristhian Guerrero Zaldivar` as the main visual signal.
- Position him as `Founder and Technical Director at FlowMind Studio`.
- Use a strong editorial portrait composition.
- Show project previews as supporting proof, especially on desktop.
- Keep the mobile version as the primary design target, not a compressed desktop layout.
- Use short, confident copy:
  - `Founder and Technical Director building premium digital presence through FlowMind Studio.`
  - `I design and build polished websites, local SEO systems, and public-facing digital experiences for real businesses that need to look trustworthy from the first scroll.`

## Navigation

- Use concise navigation labels: `Studio`, `Work`, `Capabilities`, `Contact`.
- Remove resume-first framing from the primary navigation.

## Content Cleanup

- Remove phone number, SMS links, and WhatsApp links from public UI, generated resume, PDF, and structured data.
- Remove Walmart and any work history unrelated to informatics.
- Replace traditional `Experience` framing with studio/work/practice framing where appropriate.

## Calls To Action

- Primary: `Visit FlowMind Studio`
- Secondary: `See work`
- Contact: `Email me`

## Success Criteria

- The first viewport should feel premium and current on mobile.
- The site should feel more like a creative technical studio presence than a CV.
- No phone number, WhatsApp, SMS, Walmart, or non-informatics work history should remain in public-facing content.
